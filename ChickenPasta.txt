Recipe for Chicken Pasta in the InstantPot

1. Set pot to saute
2. Add coconut oil to the pot
3. Saute the chicken (1lb)
4. Add the pasta (500g)
5. Add pasta sauce
6. Add water to cover the pasta and chicken
7. Add garlic powder, cayenne, thyme, pepper, a shake of salt
8. Cook on manual for 5 minutes
